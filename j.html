Information for Authors

Guidelines and requirements for manuscript submission

Submission Guidelines

Before submitting your manuscript, please ensure it meets the following requirements:

  • Manuscripts should be original, unpublished work not under consideration elsewhere
  • Papers must be written in English and follow the journal's formatting guidelines
  • All authors should have significantly contributed to the research
  • Proper acknowledgment of funding sources and conflicts of interest is required
  • Ethical approval for studies involving human or animal subjects must be obtained

Manuscript Preparation

Please prepare your manuscript according to the following specifications:

  • File Format: Microsoft Word (.docx) format is preferred
  • Length: Research papers should be between 4000-8000 words
  • Structure: Title, Abstract, Keywords, Introduction, Methods, Results, Discussion, Conclusion, References
  • Figures and Tables: High-resolution images (minimum 300 DPI) with captions
  • References: APA 7th edition citation style

Plagiarism Policy

ICGTETA Journal maintains a strict policy against plagiarism. All submitted manuscripts are screened using plagiarism detection software.

  • Similarity index should be less than 15%
  • Single source similarity should not exceed 5%
  • Proper citation of all sources is mandatory
  • Self-plagiarism is also considered a violation
  • Manuscripts with high similarity will be rejected without review

We recommend authors use plagiarism checking tools before submission to ensure compliance with our policy.

Review Process

Our double-blind peer review process ensures the quality and integrity of published research:

  • Initial screening by editorial team (1-2 weeks)
  • Peer review by at least two experts in the field (4-6 weeks)
  • Revision period based on reviewer comments (2-4 weeks)
  • Final decision by editor-in-chief
  • Average time from submission to decision: 8-12 weeks

Publication Fees

ICGTETA Journal charges the following publication fees to cover production costs:

  • Article Processing Charge: $250 USD
  • Waivers available for authors from developing countries
  • No submission fees
  • No charges for color figures
  • Payment is required only after manuscript acceptance
